Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

California Home Upgrades Program, operated by General Builders ("we," "us," or "our"), respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you fill out our estimate request form or contact us:

  • Full name (first and last)
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Home address and ZIP code
  • Homeownership status
  • The type of home improvement project you are interested in
  • Best time to be contacted
  • IP address, browser type, and device information (collected automatically)
  • UTM parameters and advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Click ID)

How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and confirm your free in-home assessment
  • To contact you about your estimate, financing options, and rebate programs you may qualify for
  • To dispatch a licensed contractor partner to your appointment
  • To process and follow up on your home improvement project
  • To improve our website and marketing efforts
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Consent to Communications (TCPA Compliance)

By submitting the estimate request form and checking the consent box, you expressly consent to receive calls and text messages from California Home Upgrades Program, General Builders, and our authorized contractor partners at the phone number you provided. This may include calls and texts made using an automatic telephone dialing system, prerecorded voice messages, or artificial voice.

Your consent is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may revoke your consent at any time by replying STOP to any text message, calling us at (888) 480-4286, or emailing info@californiahomeupgradesprogram.com.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Our authorized contractor partners (including General Builders) for the purpose of conducting your in-home assessment, providing an estimate, and completing the project
  • Financing partners who provide the consumer financing offered through this program (only when you elect to apply)
  • Service providers who help us operate our business (CRM, communication platforms, scheduling tools)
  • As required by law, regulation, or legal process

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights (California Residents)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@californiahomeupgradesprogram.com or call (888) 480-4286.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies, pixels, and similar tracking technologies to analyze website traffic, measure advertising effectiveness, and improve your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
