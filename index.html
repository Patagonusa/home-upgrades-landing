FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the in-home estimate really free?
Yes — 100% free, no obligation. A licensed specialist visits your home, looks at the project, walks you through every rebate program you may qualify for, and gives you a written estimate. You decide what to do next.
What's the catch with "No Payments Until 2028"?
No catch. Approved homeowners qualify for financing where the work happens now and your first monthly payment isn't due until 2028. The specialist walks through the financing options, monthly amount, and total at the appointment so you can decide with full information.
Who is the contractor that comes out?
General Builders — a California licensed and insured general contractor — performs the assessment and the work. You'll receive a confirmation text after booking, and we'll call the day before to confirm the appointment time and the contractor coming out.
Is this a government program?
No. California Home Upgrades Program is a private home-improvement campaign. We are not a government agency and are not affiliated with any government office. We do, however, review every state, federal, and utility rebate program you may qualify for during your free assessment.
Is there a senior or accessibility discount?
Yes. We offer senior pricing on bathroom accessibility upgrades like walk-in showers and tub-to-shower conversions. Your specialist will go over senior, accessibility, and SSDI-related discounts at the appointment along with any rebate programs you qualify for.
How long does the appointment take?
About one hour. The specialist arrives within a 30-minute window, walks the project with you, reviews rebates and financing, and leaves you with a written estimate. There's no pressure to decide on the spot.
